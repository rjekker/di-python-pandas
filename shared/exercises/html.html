Favorite things

  • Name: The name of the thing
  • Link: A link to a webpage (e.g. wikipedia) of that thing
  • Image: An image of that thing

Bonus: Shop

Let's create a webshop. We will start with just the structure for the site; don't worry about layout and styling right now. We can add that later.

You need at least three diffferent pages: a shopping cart and two products.

Shopping cart

This shows a list of ordered products as a table including amounts and prices.

Make sure prices are shown in bold text.

Two of the products should be clickable (links), that take you to the actual pages for those products.

Product page

For each product you create a separate page containing at least:

  • a paragraph with a description
  • a title
  • an image of the product
